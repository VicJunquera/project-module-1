ENGLISH

Geometry Wars 2 or Asteroids like game

Rectangular game board
	Press start screen
	Game over screen + restart

Player
	Movement with WASD or arrows
	Bomb with space
	Dash with Shift
	Collisions with enemies and with shots from large enemies
	3 lives

Shooting
	From the player's position
	Left click shoot to the direction where the pointer is

Score
	Point counter to defeat enemies
	Every 10,000 points +1 bomb
	From 10,000 points, greater appearance of enemies

Enemies
	Different types of behavior
	Enemy with horizontal linear advance
	Enemy with vertical linear advance
	Enemy with linear advance from random direction
	Enemy chasing the player
	Enemy Boss that moves slowly and can withstand multiple shots (larger)
		Enemy Boss Shoots

Bomb
	Eliminates all enemies (clear the board of enemies)
	Get +1 bomb when you pick up the item
